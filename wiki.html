About BTS

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan) is a South Korean boy band formed in 2013 under HYBE (BigHit Entertainment). The group has 7 members: RM, Jin, SUGA, j-hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. They are known for their powerful performances and meaningful music.

Growth Journey

BTS started small but grew into a global group through hard work, strong teamwork, and music that connects emotionally with fans worldwide.

Awards

BTS has won Billboard Awards, American Music Awards, MTV Awards, and many Korean Daesang awards. They are the first K-pop group to top the Billboard Hot 100.

Hard Work

Before debut, they trained for years with long practice hours, struggles, and rejection. Their dedication helped them become global icons.

Fun & Calm Side

BTS is funny, playful, and full of energy. At the same time, they also create calm, emotional songs about healing, love, and self-growth.
