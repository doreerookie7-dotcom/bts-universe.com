About BTS 💜

BTS is a South Korean music group known for their inspiring songs and meaningful lyrics. They started their journey in 2013 and became global artists loved by millions.

Why I like BTS ✨

They inspire me to stay strong, believe in myself, and never give up. Their music gives me comfort and motivation in my daily life.

what they mean to me🎀

I first found them during the COVID-19 period through my mom 🌸. I was just around 10 years old at that time, so I didn’t really understand what it was or what K-pop meant. I had no idea about anything back then. My elder sister helped me understand them more 💕. She gathered information about them and told me about their journey. At that time, we were seven members, so we each chose one member as our role and practiced their songs 🎤—even without knowing the meaning. Since there was no school during that time, our full-time “job” became performing their songs and even conducting little concerts in our apartment 🏠✨. As I grew older, I slowly realized how much they truly meant to me 💜. Now I am 16, and I honestly can’t imagine a single day without them. Whenever I feel down, their videos make me smile 😊, and their songs comfort me deeply. Their lives inspire and support me in ways I can’t explain. I really want to see them one day 🥺, but I can’t… and I am still waiting for that moment. I sometimes feel afraid—what if I’m too late to see them or attend their concert? 😔 If that happens, it would feel like the biggest regret of my life. To satisfy my younger self and my heart, I truly want to attend their concert and see them live one day 💜✨. I pray for it all the time, every time 🙏. I love them so much that I can’t explain it in words 💞. They were the ones who made me feel love when I had no one to give me love. When people hurt me or hated me for no reason, their words and music comforted me and healed me 💜. I love them endlessly 🌌. I will always wish for their happiness because they deserve everything they dream of—even if they wish for the moon in the sky 🌙✨, they should get it, because they truly deserve everything and more 💜.
